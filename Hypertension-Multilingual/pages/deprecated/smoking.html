Your Body Starts Healing in Minutes

Quitting smoking is the single best thing you can do for your health. The benefits begin almost immediately. Click through the timeline below to see how your body recovers when you become smoke-free.

Why is Quitting So Hard? Understanding Nicotine Addiction

Quitting is tough because nicotine is a powerful, addictive drug. Your brain gets used to it, and when you stop, you can experience withdrawal symptoms and strong cravings. Knowing what to expect is the first step to overcoming it.

These are temporary signs that your body is healing. They usually peak in the first few days and get better over a few weeks.
