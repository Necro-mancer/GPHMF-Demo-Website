Move More, Feel Better!

Regular physical activity is one of the most important things you can do for your health. It can improve brain health, help manage weight, reduce disease risk, and strengthen bones and muscles. Let's explore what counts as activity!

Your Weekly Activity Goals

For major health benefits, adults should aim for a combination of aerobic and muscle-strengthening activities each week. Here are a couple of ways to meet the CDC recommendations.

Option 1: Moderate Mix

A common and achievable goal for most adults.
