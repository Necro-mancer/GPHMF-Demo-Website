Power Your Life with Plants

Explore a vibrant way of eating that celebrates whole foods from the earth. A plant-based diet is a flexible, flavorful journey to better health.

What Does "Plant-Based" Mean?

A plant-based or plant-forward eating pattern focuses on foods primarily from plants. This includes not only fruits and vegetables, but also nuts, seeds, oils, whole grains, and legumes. It's not an all-or-nothing approach. It's a spectrum, and you can find the style that works best for you.

The Power of Plants: Key Benefits

Focusing on plants is linked to incredible health advantages and is great for the planet.

Heart Health

Lowers risk of heart disease by improving cholesterol and blood pressure.

Lower Disease Risk

Associated with a reduced risk of type 2 diabetes and certain types of cancer.

Weight Management

High fiber content helps you feel full and satisfied, aiding in weight control.

Environmental Impact

Reduces your carbon footprint and requires less land and water than animal-based diets.

Build a Perfect Plant-Powered Bowl

Creating a balanced and delicious meal is easy. Just pick a few items from each category!

1. Pick a Base

  • Quinoa
  • Brown Rice
  • Leafy Greens (Spinach, Kale)
  • Whole-Wheat Pasta

2. Add Plant Protein

  • Chickpeas
  • Black Beans
  • Lentils
  • Tofu or Tempeh

3. Load Up on Veggies

  • Roasted Broccoli
  • Bell Peppers
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Shredded Carrots

4. Finish with Flavor

  • Avocado Slices
  • Sunflower Seeds
  • Tahini Dressing
  • A Squeeze of Lemon
