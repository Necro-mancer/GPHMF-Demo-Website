DASH to a Healthier Heart

Discover the DASH eating plan, a flexible and balanced approach proven to lower blood pressure and improve heart health for life.

A Proven Plan for Your Health

DASH stands for Dietary Approaches to Stop Hypertension. It's not a fad diet, but a lifelong eating plan backed by extensive research from the National Heart, Lung, and Blood Institute (NHLBI).

The goal is to create a heart-healthy eating style that's flexible and rich in nutrients known to help lower blood pressure, such as potassium, calcium, and magnesium. It's consistently ranked as one of the best diets for overall health.

The Role of Sodium

The DASH plan offers two sodium goals. While the standard plan is effective, the lower-sodium version can have an even greater impact on blood pressure.

Standard DASH

2,300mg

The standard daily limit, which is still lower than what many people typically consume.

Lower Sodium DASH

1,500mg

An even more effective goal for lowering blood pressure, recommended for many adults.

Your 7-Day DASH Kickstart Plan

Start with small, gradual changes. Use this checklist to build healthy habits one day at a time. Click to mark your progress!

  • Day 1: Add a serving of vegetables to your lunch and dinner.
  • Day 2: Switch to whole-wheat bread for your sandwiches or toast.
  • Day 3: Have a piece of fruit as your afternoon snack instead of chips or cookies.
  • Day 4: Use a salt-free seasoning blend on your food instead of reaching for the salt shaker.
  • Day 5: Choose low-fat or fat-free milk or yogurt.
  • Day 6: Have a handful of unsalted nuts as a snack.
  • Day 7: Take a 15-minute walk after dinner. You did it!
